Mac n Cheese

Mac n Cheese is basically just a cheesy macaroni. It's quick and simple, you can mix it with any kind of cheese you like

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
